    Master of Technology

    Our Two-Year M.Tech programs are designed to provide advanced technical expertise, fostering a deep understanding of complex engineering concepts. Built for innovators and future industry leaders, these programs combine rigorous coursework with extensive research and development opportunities.

    Structural Engineering

    Advanced structural analysis, design of complex structures, earthquake engineering, and construction technologies.

    Specialization Areas:
    Advanced Structural Analysis Earthquake Resistant Design Prestressed Concrete Steel Structure Design Bridge Engineering Construction Management
    2 Years
    20 Seats
    B.Tech Required

    Thermal Engineering

    Heat transfer, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, energy systems, and advanced thermal management technologies.

    Specialization Areas:
    Advanced Heat Transfer Computational Fluid Dynamics Renewable Energy Systems HVAC System Design Thermal Power Plant Engg. Energy Efficiency
    2 Years
    20 Seats
    B.Tech Required

    Power System & Automation

    Smart grid technologies, power system protection, industrial automation, and renewable energy integration.

    Specialization Areas:
    Smart Grid Technologies Power System Protection Renewable Energy Integration Power Quality Management Industrial Automation SCADA Systems
    2 Years
    20 Seats
    B.Tech Required

    Digital Communication

    Advanced communication systems, signal processing, high-speed wireless networks, and digital signal processing.

    Specialization Areas:
    Digital Signal Processing Wireless Comm. Systems Optical Communication Network Security Satellite Communication 5G & Beyond Tech
    2 Years
    20 Seats
    B.Tech Required

    IoT & Sensor Systems

    Internet of Things, complex sensor networks, advanced embedded systems, and smart system architecture design.

    Specialization Areas:
    IoT Architecture Wireless Sensor Networks Embedded System Design Smart City Tech Industrial IoT Edge Computing & AI
    2 Years
    20 Seats
    B.Tech Required
