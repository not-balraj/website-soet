    Who Can Apply

    Eligibility Criteria

    Detailed requirements for each program. Meeting these criteria is the first step to securing your seat through MPDTE counseling.

    M.Tech Program

    Master of Technology

    Qualification B.E. / B.Tech in the relevant engineering discipline from a recognized university / institution
    Min. Marks 55% aggregate (50% for SC/ST/OBC candidates of MP)
    Entrance Exam Valid GATE score in the relevant paper is mandatory. GATE is valid for 3 years from the date of result.
    Counseling Through MPDTE (dte.mponline.gov.in). Non-GATE applicants may apply in CLC rounds if seats remain.
    Specialization Match between B.Tech branch and M.Tech specialization is recommended — check seat matrix for detailed branch mapping.
    Ph.D. Program

    Doctoral Program

    Qualification M.Tech / M.E. in relevant engineering discipline OR M.Sc. in relevant science subject from a recognized university
    Min. Marks 55% aggregate in qualifying degree (50% for SC/ST candidates)
    Selection Process Written research aptitude test + personal interview with the Doctoral Committee. GATE/NET scoreholders may be exempted from the written test.
    Mode Options Full-time (eligible for fellowship) and Sponsored Part-time (for working professionals with employer NOC)
    Fellowship Full-time scholars may be eligible for UGC, CSIR, or institute fellowships based on GATE score and research area
    How to Apply

    The Admission Journey

    A clear, step-by-step pathway from your GATE exam to your first day at SOET.

    🏅
    1

    Clear GATE in Your Discipline

    Appear in GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) in the paper relevant to your chosen M.Tech specialization — CE for Structural, ME for Thermal, EE for Power Systems.

    🌐
    2

    Register on MPDTE Portal

    Create your account at dte.mponline.gov.in, fill the application form, upload your GATE scorecard, B.Tech marksheets, and category certificates, and pay the counseling registration fee.

    🎯
    3

    Choice Filling & Allotment

    Enter your preferred institute-specialization combinations in priority order. Seat allotment is done on GATE rank, category, and seat availability. Multiple counseling rounds are conducted.

    ✅
    4

    Report to SOET & Enroll

    Visit SOET campus with original documents for physical verification. Pay your semester fees, complete enrollment formalities, and confirm your seat to begin your M.Tech journey.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Answers to the most common queries from M.Tech and Ph.D. applicants. Don't see your question? Reach out directly.

    Is GATE mandatory for M.Tech admission?

    +
    GATE is the primary and preferred route for M.Tech admission through MPDTE counseling. GATE scoreholders are given priority in all counseling rounds. However, if seats remain vacant after all GATE-based rounds, MPDTE may open College-Level Counseling (CLC) where non-GATE applicants with a strong B.Tech aggregate may apply. It is strongly recommended to appear in GATE for a secure admission.

    Can I pursue M.Tech without an MP domicile?

    +
    Yes. A portion of M.Tech seats are designated as All India quota seats, open to students from any state. However, most seats are under the MP state quota. Students from other states are advised to check the MPDTE M.Tech seat matrix annually for the exact number of All India seats available per specialization.

    What GATE papers are accepted for each specialization?

    +
    The GATE paper must align with your chosen specialization: CE (Civil Engineering) for Structural Engineering, ME (Mechanical Engineering) for Thermal Engineering, and EE (Electrical Engineering) for Power Systems. Candidates with interdisciplinary B.Tech degrees should contact the admissions office to confirm eligibility for specific specializations.

    Is there a fellowship or stipend for M.Tech students?

    +
    GATE-qualified M.Tech students admitted through MPDTE counseling are eligible for the AICTE stipend (subject to government rules and continuation of the scheme). Ph.D. scholars may additionally be eligible for UGC JRF, CSIR JRF, or institute fellowships based on their GATE score and the research project they are assigned to. Please confirm the current stipend status at the time of admission.

    How do I apply for the Ph.D. program?

    +
    Ph.D. admissions at SOET are conducted through a separate process from M.Tech MPDTE counseling. Applicants must: (1) Check for Ph.D. advertisement on the official university website, (2) Submit an online application form during the notified period, (3) Appear in a written research aptitude test (GATE/NET holders may be exempted), and (4) Attend a personal interview with the Doctoral Committee. Successful candidates are issued an admission letter.

    Are hostel facilities available for M.Tech students?

    +
    Yes. Hostel accommodation is available for M.Tech students on campus, subject to seat availability. M.Tech students are allotted rooms separately from B.Tech students on a first-come, first-served basis post-admission. Hostel fees are collected separately from tuition fees. Contact the Dean's Office or fill the enquiry form below for the current hostel fee structure and availability.
