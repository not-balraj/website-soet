The Pride of the University: National Cadet Corps

Discipline • Leadership • Honour

University Unit

Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya is proud to host a prestigious unit of the NCC (Army Wing), drawing dedicated cadets from across all university departments and institutes. Enrollment is highly competitive, focusing on physical prowess, mental fortitude, and a commitment to military discipline. Our cadets don't just train; they excel at the highest level of national camps representing the entire university.
