    Admission Criteria

    Eligibility Requirements

    You must meet all eligibility criteria before registering for MPDTE counseling. Check each requirement carefully against your marksheets and certificates.

    Academic

    Qualification Criteria

    Qualification
    10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM) from a recognized board
    Min. Marks (General)
    45% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics combined
    Min. Marks (SC/ST/OBC)
    40% aggregate in PCM — relaxation as per MP Government norms
    Entrance Exam
    JEE Main 2026 (primary route) or MPDTE State Counseling rank
    Age Limit
    Minimum 17 years as on 31st December 2026. No upper age limit.
    Appearing Candidates
    Students appearing in 12th board exams may apply provisionally, subject to meeting eligibility upon declaration of results
    Domicile & Quota

    Seat Quota Eligibility

    State Quota
    Madhya Pradesh domicile certificate mandatory. Covers approximately 85% of total seats.
    All India Quota
    Open to students from any Indian state — no domicile requirement. Approximately 15% of seats.
    SC / ST Quota
    Valid caste certificate issued by MP Government authority required. Separate merit list maintained.
    OBC (NCL) Quota
    Non-Creamy Layer OBC certificate not older than one year from the date of counseling registration
    EWS Quota
    Economically Weaker Section certificate issued by competent authority for current year required
    PWD / Divyangjan
    Horizontal reservation as per RPWD Act 2016. Disability certificate from CMO required.
    Step-by-Step Guide

    How to Secure Your B.Tech Seat

    SOET admits B.Tech students exclusively through the MPDTE centralized counseling process. Here is exactly what you need to do, in order.

    Appear in JEE Main 2026 Jan – Apr 2026

    Register and appear in JEE Main Session 1 or Session 2 conducted by NTA. Your best percentile across sessions will be considered. Download and safely store your scorecard — it is the primary document for counseling.

    Register at jeemain.nta.nic.in before the deadline
    Download and keep your JEE Main scorecard (Application No. + DOB required)
    Both sessions permitted — best score is considered automatically
    NTA JEE Main Portal
    02

    Register on MPDTE Portal May 2026

    Once JEE Main results are declared, register on the official MPDTE counseling portal. Fill in your personal details, upload scanned documents, and pay the counseling registration fee online.

    Visit dte.mponline.gov.in and create an account
    Upload 10th, 12th marksheets, JEE scorecard, Aadhaar & category certificate
    Pay counseling fee (₹500 for General, ₹250 for SC/ST) via online payment
    MPDTE Counseling Portal
    03

    Fill Choices & Lock Preferences May – Jun 2026

    Log into your MPDTE account during the choice-filling window. Add your preferred institute-branch combinations in order of priority. You can add, reorder, and delete choices — but once you lock and submit, no changes are allowed.

    Add SOET Ujjain as your preferred institute with your desired branch
    Fill multiple choices in order of priority (recommended: 10–15 options)
    Lock choices before deadline — unlocked choices will not be considered
    04

    Seat Allotment Results Jun 2026

    MPDTE will publish seat allotment results round-by-round. Check your allotment letter in your portal account. If allotted SOET, download the allotment letter and proceed to the next step within the deadline.

    Download allotment letter from MPDTE portal immediately after results
    Options: Accept & Freeze / Accept & Float / Accept & Slide — choose wisely
    Multiple rounds conducted — missing one round means forfeiting the allotment
    05

    Document Verification at SOET Jun – Jul 2026

    Report to the SOET admissions office on your allotted date with all original documents and self-attested photocopies. Your documents will be physically verified by our admissions committee.

    Bring all originals + 2 sets of self-attested photocopies of each document
    Refer to the document checklist below — missing documents = cancelled seat
    Medical fitness certificate must be from a registered government doctor
    View Document Checklist
    06

    Fee Payment & Enrollment Jul 2026

    Pay the first semester tuition fee and complete your enrollment formalities at SOET. Receive your enrollment number, student ID card, and class schedule. Your B.Tech journey officially begins!

    Pay semester fee at the SOET accounts office (cash / DD / online)
    Fill enrollment form and submit passport photographs
    Collect student ID card and timetable from respective department

    Financial Aid

    Scholarships & Fee Waivers

    Multiple government scholarship schemes cover partial or full tuition for eligible students. Apply for all schemes you qualify for — they can be stacked in some cases.

    MP Post-Matric Scholarship
    SC / ST / OBC Students
    Madhya Pradesh government's post-matric scholarship for students from reserved categories. Covers full or partial tuition fee directly credited to the college. Renewal required each year with academic performance proof.
    Up to full tuition fee covered
    AICTE PRAGATI Scholarship
    Girl Students — All Categories
    AICTE's scholarship for girl students pursuing technical education. Provides ₹50,000 per year (tuition + incidentals). One girl student per family is eligible. Family income must be below ₹8 lakh per annum.
    ₹50,000 per year
    AICTE SAKSHAM Scholarship
    Specially-Abled Students
    AICTE's scholarship for differently-abled students (40%+ disability). Covers ₹50,000 per year. Disability certificate from the Chief Medical Officer (CMO) of the district is required for application.
    ₹50,000 per year
    EWS / Merit Scholarship
    General Category — Income-based & Merit
    Economically Weaker Section scholarship from MP Government and university-level merit scholarships for top-performing General category students. Criteria and amounts vary — contact the scholarship cell at SOET for the latest details.
    Varies — apply each academic year
    Pro Tip: Apply for all scholarship schemes you are eligible for simultaneously — they are not mutually exclusive in all cases. Our admissions office will help you navigate the application process. Scholarship amounts are credited directly to your bank account or adjusted against fees.

    Common Questions

    B.Tech Admissions FAQ

    Answers to the questions we receive most often from prospective students and their parents.

    Is JEE Main mandatory for B.Tech admission at SOET?

    +
    JEE Main is the primary and recommended route. Admissions are conducted through the MPDTE counseling process, which uses JEE Main rank as the primary merit criterion. However, in later counseling rounds (Round 3 onwards), students who have not appeared in JEE Main may be considered based on 12th board marks, subject to seat availability. We strongly recommend appearing in JEE Main for the best chances.

    What is the cutoff JEE Main percentile for ECS at SOET?

    +
    Cutoffs vary every year based on the number of applicants and seat availability. For ECS (the most popular branch), the General category cutoff has typically been in the 60th–75th percentile range in recent years. SC/ST/OBC cutoffs are significantly lower. We recommend checking the official MPDTE closing rank data from previous years on their portal for exact figures.

    Can I change my branch after getting admission?

    +
    Branch change is permitted after the completion of Semester 1, subject to availability of seats in the desired branch, your CGPA (minimum 7.5 CGPA typically required), and university regulations. It is not guaranteed — so choose your preferred branch carefully during counseling. Branch change requests are processed by the academic section at the start of the second semester.

    What if I miss all MPDTE rounds — can I still get admission?

    +
    Yes — after all MPDTE counseling rounds are completed, any remaining vacant seats are filled through College-Level Counseling (CLC). During CLC, you can apply directly to SOET's admissions office. CLC typically happens in late July. Fill our enquiry form below and we will guide you through the process as soon as CLC dates are announced by MPDTE.

    Are hostel facilities guaranteed after admission?

    +
    SOET provides separate hostel facilities for male and female students on the Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya campus. Hostel rooms are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis after confirmation of admission. While the university makes every effort to accommodate all applicants, hostel availability is not guaranteed due to limited capacity. Early payment and enrollment increases your chances of getting hostel accommodation.

    Does SOET offer lateral entry (direct second year) admission?

    +
    Yes. Diploma holders from recognized polytechnics can seek lateral entry directly into the second year (3rd semester) of B.Tech through the MPDTE Lateral Entry Counseling process. The eligibility is a 3-year Diploma in an appropriate engineering discipline with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST). Lateral entry seats are over and above the regular intake and are processed separately by MPDTE.

    What are the medium of instruction and examination?

    +
    The medium of instruction and examination at SOET is English for all technical subjects. Some elective and social science subjects may offer Hindi as a medium option. All lab reports, projects, and final exams are conducted in English. Students from Hindi-medium backgrounds can adjust — our faculty is supportive and the curriculum is designed to ease the transition.
