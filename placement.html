    Recruitment Procedure

    1
    Invitation: Placement Cell sends official invitations to companies along with student data and profiles.
    2
    Pre-Placement Talk (PPT): Companies interact with students to introduce their culture, roles, and expectations.
    3
    Selection Process: Written Tests, Group Discussions, and Personal Interviews are conducted on campus or online.
    4
    Final Offer: Results are declared, and appointment letters are routed through the T&P Cell.
