    The Recruitment Process

    A streamlined, transparent, and efficient four-step pathway connecting our top-tier engineering talent with global industry leaders.

    01

    Invitation

    The placement cell initiates contact by sending formal invitations alongside our comprehensive placement brochure to prospective recruiters.

    02

    Pre-Placement Talk

    Companies engage with students through on-campus or virtual sessions, detailing company profiles, job roles, and career growth trajectories.

    03

    Screening & Interviews

    A rigorous selection process commences, including aptitude assessments, group discussions, and specialized technical & HR interviews.

    04

    Final Offer

    Successful candidates are formally extended job offers, coordinated directly through the Training and Placement Cell to ensure transparency.
