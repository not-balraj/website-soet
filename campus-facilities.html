    World-Class Infrastructure for Future Engineers

    SOET is spread across a lush green campus within Vikram University, offering a blend of traditional academic values and modern technological resources. From high-tech laboratories to massive libraries and sports complexes, we provide everything a student needs for a balanced campus life.

    Smart Classrooms

    Our lecture halls are equipped with audio-visual aids, projectors, and ergonomic seating to facilitate an interactive learning environment.

    Maharaja Vikramaditya Library

    Access to thousands of volumes, international journals, and a dedicated digital section with NPTEL and IEEE resources.

    View Library Details →

    Advanced IT Center

    High-speed 1Gbps internet connectivity, latest software suites, and 24/7 Wi-Fi access across the engineering blocks.

    University Auditorium

    A massive indoor seating facility used for national conferences, seminars, and the grand "Vikramotsav" annual fest.

    Health Centre

    On-campus medical assistance with ambulance services and regular health check-up camps for students and staff.

    Transport Facility

    University-run bus services connecting the campus to all major parts of Ujjain city for day scholars.
