Scholarships & Financial Aid

Eligible students can avail themselves of various government and university scholarships.

MMVY (Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana)

A flagship scheme by the MP Government providing full fee waivers for meritorious students (scoring 70%+ in MP Board or 85%+ in CBSE/ICSE) with a qualifying JEE rank.

Post-Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC)

Financial assistance provided by the Government of Madhya Pradesh for students belonging to reserved categories based on family income criteria.

Mukhya Mantri Jan Kalyan (Sambal)

Fee waiver scheme for students whose parents are registered under the unorganized sector workers' scheme by the State Government.

GATE Stipend (For M.Tech)

Admitted M.Tech students with a valid GATE score are eligible to apply for the AICTE PG Scholarship (stipend of ₹12,400 per month) subject to AICTE rules.
