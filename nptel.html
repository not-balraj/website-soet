Expand your knowledge with free online courses from premier institutions.

SWAYAM-NPTEL Local Chapter

Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya is an active Local Chapter of NPTEL (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning). Under the AICTE mandate, students are encouraged to enroll in online certification courses offered by IITs and IISc to enhance their technical knowledge and earn academic credits.

Recognized Local Chapter

Credit Transfer

Up to 20% of academic credits can be earned via MOOCs.

E-Certificates

Official certificates from IITs/IISc upon exam completion.

Employability

Industry-recognized skills for better placement opportunities.

Credit Transfer Guidelines

  • 8-Week Course: Equivalent to 2 Academic Credits.
  • 12-Week Course: Equivalent to 3 Academic Credits.
  • Students must score a minimum of 40% in assignments and 40% in the final proctored exam to be eligible for credit transfer.
  • Only courses approved by the respective Department Head will be considered for credit mapping against university electives.
