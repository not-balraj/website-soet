Vikramotsav

The Grand Annual Socio-Cultural & Technical Festival

Celebrating Heritage & Innovation

Vikramotsav is more than just a fest; it is a tradition at Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya. It brings together the ancient cultural soul of Ujjain and the modern technical spirit of SOET. From classical dance and poetry to high-end coding battles and robotics, the fest is a 3-day extravaganza of talent.

Cultural Night

Featuring "Sur-Sandhya", folk dance competitions, and the star-studded celebrity concert on the final night.

  • Inter-College Dance Battle
  • Fashion Walk (Ethno-Couture)
  • War of Bands

Tech-A-Thon (SOET)

Organized by Code-D-Code and SOET departments, focusing on innovation and problem-solving.

  • 24-Hour Hackathon
  • Robo-Sumo Wrestling
  • Bridge-It (Civil Design)

Literary Events

A tribute to the literary history of Ujjain and Kalidas, featuring debates and poetry slams.

  • Youth Parliament
  • Hindi/English Poetry Slam
  • Mega Quiz Championship
