Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our admission process, eligibility criteria, counseling, and campus facilities. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our admission cell.

Undergraduate (B.Tech) Admissions

What is the basic eligibility for B.Tech admission?

Candidates must have passed their 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subject. You need a minimum of 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories of Madhya Pradesh).

Do I need to give JEE Main to get admission?

While JEE Main is not strictly mandatory for all seats, it is highly recommended. The first phases of MPDTE centralized counseling strictly prioritize students with a valid JEE Main score. If seats remain vacant, admissions are then done based on 12th-grade merit (College Level Counseling).

What is the College Level Counseling (CLC) process?

CLC is conducted at the institute level after the online DTE counseling rounds are completed. Students can apply directly at the SOET campus for any remaining vacant seats based on their 12th standard PCM percentage.

Postgraduate (M.Tech) Admissions

What are the eligibility criteria for M.Tech?

Candidates must hold a B.E./B.Tech degree in a relevant discipline from a recognized university with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST/OBC of Madhya Pradesh).

Is a GATE score compulsory for M.Tech?

Admission is primarily based on valid GATE scores through MPDTE counseling. However, if seats remain vacant after GATE-qualified candidates are admitted, non-GATE candidates can be admitted based on their B.Tech graduation percentage.

General & Facilities

Does the college provide hostel facilities?

Yes, SOET provides secure, well-furnished, and separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls, complete with mess facilities, Wi-Fi, and 24/7 security.

Can I apply for government scholarships?

Absolutely. Eligible students can apply for state and central government scholarships such as MMVY (Medhavi), Post-Matric Scholarships for SC/ST/OBC, and the Sambal Yojana. Our accounts department assists students with the portal registration process.

Do you have a dedicated placement cell?

Yes, our Corporate Resource Center (CRC) actively trains students in aptitude, coding, and soft skills from the second year onwards, and brings top IT and core engineering companies to the campus for recruitment drives.
