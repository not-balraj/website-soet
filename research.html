    Message from the Dean (R&D)

    The Office of Research and Development at the School of Engineering and Technology (SOET), Vikram University, is committed to fostering a vibrant ecosystem of fundamental and translational research. Much like the premier technical institutes of the nation, our mandate is to push the boundaries of human knowledge while addressing the pressing socio-economic and technological challenges of our time.

    We strongly encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, uniting faculty and scholars from Computer Science, Core Engineering, and Applied Sciences. Through robust academia-industry partnerships, government-sponsored initiatives, and a dedicated intellectual property framework, we ensure that the novel ideas germinating in our laboratories successfully transition into deployed technologies that benefit society at large.

    "Research is not merely an academic exercise; it is the engine of national progress. We invite brilliant minds, visionary industry leaders, and policy-makers to partner with us in this pursuit of excellence."

    Sponsored Projects and Grants

    Central Research Facilities (CRF)

    To support high-end research, SOET houses centralized laboratories equipped with sophisticated, capital-intensive analytical instruments. These facilities operate on a centralized booking system, ensuring access for all interdisciplinary research teams, postgraduate scholars, and partnered industrial entities.

    High-Performance Computing (HPC) Lab

    Equipped with state-of-the-art GPU clusters and multi-node parallel processing setups for complex fluid dynamics, AI model training, and cryptographic research.

    Advanced Materials & Characterization

    Housing precision instruments such as X-Ray Diffractometers (XRD), Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), and Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) setups.

    Microwave & VLSI Center

    Featuring advanced spectrum analyzers, FPGA development boards, and anechoic chambers for next-generation telecommunication protocols and antenna design.

    Incubation Center & E-Cell

    The Vikram Innovation and Incubation Center acts as a catalyst for budding entrepreneurs. We believe that researchers should be job creators, not just job seekers. The center provides a comprehensive support mechanism to convert academic prototypes into commercially viable products.

    • Seed Funding: Facilitating early-stage capital through government startup schemes and alumni angel networks.
    • Mentorship: Dedicated guidance from industry veterans regarding business strategy, legal compliance, and market scaling.
    • Workspace Infrastructure: Providing fully furnished co-working spaces, high-speed internet, and rapid prototyping tools (3D printers, CNC machines).
    • Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell): A student-run body that organizes annual hackathons, B-plan competitions, and speaker sessions featuring successful unicorn founders.
