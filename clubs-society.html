    Clubs & Societies

    Discover the vibrant communities that make life at SOET exciting and enriching.

    Fuel Your Passion

    Life at SOET and Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya isn't just about lectures and labs. Our vibrant club culture allows you to lead, innovate, and collaborate. From high-octane coding challenges to cultural festivals, there is a community for everyone.

    SOET Departmental Clubs

    Code-D-Code

    TECHNICAL

    The premier coding community of SOET. Focused on competitive programming, web development, and preparing students for tech hackathons.

    Meets: Every Saturday

    Robo-Tech Society

    COMING SOON

    An upcoming initiative for hardware enthusiasts, focusing on Arduino projects, Drone tech, and Automation.

    University-Wide Societies

    Students of SOET also have full access to the central societies of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya, which bring together students from all disciplines.

    Literary & Cultural Society

    Organizers of the annual university fest, youth festivals, and inter-college debate competitions.

    University Sports Board

    Promoting excellence in Athletics, Cricket, Football, and Indoor sports at state and national levels.

    Eco Club / NSS

    Dedicated to environmental awareness, tree plantation drives, and social service in rural Ujjain.

    Fine Arts Society

    For students interested in painting, photography, and sculpture. Regular exhibitions on campus.

    Want to start a new club?

    The Office of the Dean supports student-led initiatives. Submit your proposal today!
