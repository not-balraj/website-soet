Fuel Your Passion

Life at SOET and Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya isn't just about lectures and labs. Our vibrant club culture allows you to lead, innovate, and collaborate. From high-octane coding challenges to cultural festivals, there is a community for everyone.

Students of SOET also have full access to the central societies of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya, which bring together students from all disciplines.
