    About SOET

    Engineering the future through uncompromising standards, rigorous academics, and a relentless pursuit of excellence since 2011.

    Pioneering the Future
    of Engineering.

    The School of Engineering & Technology (SOET) is a premier University Teaching Department of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya, Ujjain. We do not just teach syllabus; we forge innovators. Sanctioned by the AICTE and the Department of Technical Education (MP), SOET offers robust undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and brutal industry demands.

    Our graduates do not simply enter the workforce—they arrive ready to lead, solve complex problems, and dictate the pace of technological advancement.

    Our Vision

    To be a globally recognized center of excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation, producing technically sound and socially responsible professionals who can lead the technological advancement of society.

    Our Mission

    • Impart high-quality technical education through modern pedagogical methods.
    • Foster a relentless culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
    • Establish ironclad industry-academia collaborations.
    • Inculcate strong ethical values and leadership qualities.

    Our History

    A Legacy of Growth

    2011

    The Foundation

    Establishment of the School of Engineering and Technology under the prestigious faculty of Engineering at Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya.

    2015

    First Graduating Class

    The first batch of B.Tech students successfully graduates, establishing a strong precedent with 70% securing campus placements in top-tier firms.

    2018

    Postgraduate Expansion

    Introduction of cutting-edge Postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in Structural and Thermal Engineering to foster advanced research capabilities.

    2023

    Accreditation & Innovation

    SOET awarded an 'A+' grade in the recent NAAC accreditation cycle, marked alongside the inauguration of the new state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence Lab.
