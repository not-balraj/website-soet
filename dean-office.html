    Message from the Dean

    Welcome to the School of Engineering and Technology! The Office of the Dean (Student Welfare) is dedicated to ensuring that your time at the university is enriching, safe, and holistic. We believe that true engineering excellence is achieved not just inside the classroom, but through active participation in campus life, leadership roles, and community service.

    Our office serves as the central hub for all student activities, grievance redressal, extracurricular clubs, and personal counseling. We are here to support your mental, physical, and academic well-being. My door is always open to students who need guidance, have innovative ideas for the campus, or require support navigating university life.

    Wishing you a transformative journey at SOET!

    Student Services & Support

    The Dean's Office manages a wide array of student-centric initiatives and administrative approvals.

    Discipline & Anti-Ragging

    Maintaining a zero-tolerance policy against ragging and ensuring a safe, respectful campus environment for all students.

    Clubs & Societies

    Overseeing the formation, funding, and events of all technical, cultural, and sports clubs managed by the Student Council.

    Counseling & Wellness

    Providing confidential psychological counseling and mentorship to help students manage academic and personal stress.

    Scholarships & Approvals

    Facilitating state and central government scholarship verifications, bonafide certificates, and financial aid processing.

    Hostel & Mess Welfare

    Coordinating with Chief Wardens to ensure quality living conditions, hygiene, and proper dining facilities in university hostels.

    Grievance Redressal

    A dedicated portal and committee to address student complaints regarding academics, facilities, or peer disputes.

    Need Assistance? Reach Out to Us.

    The Office of the Dean is located on the Ground Floor, Administrative Block.
