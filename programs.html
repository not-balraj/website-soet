    Academic Disciplines

    The School of Engineering and Technology offers industry-aligned programs designed to produce globally competitive engineers, researchers, and innovators. Explore our undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral offerings below.

    Undergraduate Programs

    B.Tech (4 Years)

    Electrical Engineering

    Focuses on power generation, electrical machines, control systems, and renewable energy integration.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Electronics and Computer Science

    A hybrid branch bridging hardware and software, covering embedded systems, programming, and architecture.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Electronics and Communication

    Dive into microprocessors, signal processing, VLSI design, and modern telecommunication networks.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Mechanical Engineering

    Master thermodynamics, robotics, fluid mechanics, and advanced automotive systems design.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Civil Engineering

    Learn structural design, modern construction materials, environmental engineering, and smart city planning.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Agricultural Engineering

    Combines engineering with agriculture, focusing on farm machinery, soil conservation, and crop processing.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Dairy Technology

    Specialized study in dairy plant design, processing technologies, quality control, and bioprocess engineering.

    8 Semesters Intake: 60

    Postgraduate Programs

    M.Tech (2 Years)

    Structural Engineering

    Advanced research and design techniques for earthquake-resistant, high-rise, and sustainable structures.

    4 Semesters Intake: 18

    Thermal Engineering

    In-depth study of heat transfer, fluid dynamics, refrigeration, and renewable energy systems.

    4 Semesters Intake: 18

    Power System and Automation

    Focuses on smart grid technologies, high voltage engineering, and automated power distribution networks.

    4 Semesters Intake: 18

    Digital Communication

    Advanced signal processing, wireless networks, optical communication, and modern coding theory.

    4 Semesters Intake: 18

    IoT and Sensor System

    Cutting-edge research in sensor networks, smart devices, embedded automation, and big data interfaces.

    4 Semesters Intake: 18
