    Academic Programs

    Engineering excellence across seven disciplines — undergraduate, postgraduate & doctoral pathways.
    Our ProgramsAICTE Approved  ·  Est. 2011
    SOET offers three tiers of engineering education — a four-year B.Tech, a two-year M.Tech, and a doctoral Ph.D — each designed to build deep technical expertise and real-world readiness.
    UG
    Undergraduate

    Bachelor of Technology
    (B.Tech)

    A rigorous four-year program spanning eight semesters. Students choose from seven engineering branches and build a strong foundation in theory, laboratory work, and industry-oriented projects.
    4 Years / 8 Semesters 7 Branches 540 Seats
    Explore B.Tech
    PG
    Postgraduate

    Master of Technology
    (M.Tech)

    A two-year postgraduate program focused on advanced specialization, research methodology, and cutting-edge engineering practice — ideal for those seeking deeper expertise or a path towards academia.
    2 Years / 4 Semesters 3 Specializations 54 Seats
    Explore M.Tech

    Accreditation & RecognitionQuality Assured
    SOET's programs are backed by statutory approvals and quality recognitions that affirm our commitment to engineering education standards.
    AICTEAll India Council for Technical EducationAll B.Tech and M.Tech programs are AICTE approved, ensuring national-standard curriculum, faculty norms, and infrastructure.
    NAACNational Assessment & Accreditation CouncilSamrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya is NAAC accredited, validating quality in teaching, research, and governance.
    NIRFNational Institutional Ranking FrameworkRanked by the Ministry of Education under NIRF, reflecting performance in teaching, research, outreach, and perception.
    UGCUniversity Grants CommissionThe university holds UGC recognition under Sec. 2(f) & 12(B), ensuring degrees are valid for government employment and higher studies.
    DTE MPDirectorate of Technical Education, MPAdmissions are conducted through DTE Madhya Pradesh via state counselling, ensuring fair and transparent selection.
