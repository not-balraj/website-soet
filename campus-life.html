Annual Celebrations

The SOET Festival Calendar

Three signature events define the SOET academic year. From a university-wide cultural extravaganza to a national-level tech competition — these are the days the entire campus lives for.

Add Vikramotsav fest photo here · images/cl-vikramotsav.jpg

Vikramotsav

The flagship annual cultural and technical fest of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya. Three days of music, dance, drama, hackathons, debate, and competitions that attract thousands of participants from colleges across Madhya Pradesh.

Explore Fests
Technovate

SOET's flagship tech competition — robotics, hackathons, project exhibitions, and paper presentations. Open to all engineering colleges nationally.

Cultural Fest

Classical dance, folk music, street plays, singing competitions, and fashion shows celebrating the cultural diversity of SOET's student community.

Annual Sports Meet

A week-long inter-departmental athletics and sports event with competitions in 15+ sports, culminating in an awards ceremony and prize distribution.
