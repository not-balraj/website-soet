    Home / Anti-Ragging

    Zero Tolerance for Ragging

    Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited in the School of Engineering and Technology. SOET is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment where every student can learn with dignity and confidence.

    What Is Ragging?

    Ragging includes any act of physical abuse, verbal abuse, psychological harassment, intimidation, humiliation, or coercion by senior students that causes discomfort, fear, or harm to juniors.

    Punishable Offence

    As per UGC regulations, strict disciplinary actions are taken against offenders, including suspension, expulsion, fines, and legal proceedings under applicable laws.

    Report Immediately

    Any incident of ragging must be reported without delay. Confidential support and prompt action are ensured by the institute anti-ragging committee.

    Helpline & Contact
