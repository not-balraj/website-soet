Rules & Regulations

Academic Policies

Key rules that govern academic conduct, attendance, grading, and progression at SOET. Every enrolled student is expected to be familiar with these.

Attendance Requirements

+
A minimum of 75% attendance in each subject (theory + lab) is mandatory to be eligible to appear in the University End-Semester Examination. Students falling below 75% will be detained and will not be permitted to appear in the exams for the concerned subject. Medical leaves can be condoned up to a maximum of 10% upon submission of valid documentation approved by the Dean's office. Attendance is calculated separately for theory and lab components.

Grading System & CGPA

+
SOET follows the 10-point CGPA grading system as prescribed by Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya. Internal assessment contributes 30 marks and the University End-Semester Examination contributes 70 marks per subject. The grade thresholds are: O (Outstanding) ≥ 90, A+ ≥ 80, A ≥ 70, B+ ≥ 60, B ≥ 55, C ≥ 50, D ≥ 45, F < 45. Students scoring F in any subject must appear for the supplementary examination.

Re-Evaluation & Re-Checking Policy

+
Students who are dissatisfied with their result may apply for re-checking (marks re-totalling) within 15 days of result declaration. Re-evaluation (full re-assessment of answer script) is also available at the university level with an applicable fee. Applications must be submitted to the Examination Cell with the prescribed fee payment receipt. Results of re-evaluation are final and binding. Contact the exam cell for current fee schedules and procedures.

NPTEL Credit Transfer Policy

+
Students who complete NPTEL online courses with a minimum of 60% in the proctored examination are eligible for credit transfer as per the AICTE NPTEL credit transfer guidelines. A maximum of 4 credits per semester and 20 credits over the entire degree program may be transferred. The NPTEL Local Chapter coordinator at SOET must be notified before the course starts, and the certificate must be submitted to the academic section within 30 days of result declaration.

Branch Change Policy

+
Branch change requests are processed at the end of the first semester, subject to availability of vacant seats in the desired branch. Eligibility criteria include a minimum CGPA of 7.5 in Semester 1, no active backlogs, and 75%+ overall attendance. The number of available seats for branch change is limited (typically 5% of sanctioned intake). Requests are processed in order of CGPA merit. Branch change is a one-time provision — no further change is permitted.

Academic Integrity & Anti-Plagiarism

+
SOET maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy on academic dishonesty. Plagiarism in project reports, theses, assignments, and lab records is checked using anti-plagiarism tools. A similarity index above 20% results in rejection and mandatory resubmission. Copying in examinations or presenting another's work as one's own may result in suspension or cancellation of results for the entire semester. All final-year projects are screened before submission to the university.
