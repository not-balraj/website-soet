Welcome to the Knowledge Resource Centre

The School of Engineering and Technology (SOET) is enriched by a dual-library system. Students have exclusive access to the specialized SOET Departmental Library for immediate academic needs, as well as full membership to the historic Central Library of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya.

Maharaja Jiwajirao Central Library

Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya

Established as the primary knowledge repository for the university, the Central Library is a massive complex housing hundreds of thousands of volumes, rare manuscripts, and a sprawling reading hall. It serves as the ultimate research destination for PhD scholars and advanced B.Tech/M.Tech students.

SOET Campus
