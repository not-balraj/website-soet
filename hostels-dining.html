    Residential Life at Vikram University

    The university provides separate, well-maintained hostel facilities for boys and girls to ensure a safe and focused academic environment. With multiple blocks and dedicated dining halls, students enjoy a comfortable "home away from home" experience.

    Boys' Hostels (Separate Complex)

    BLOCK 1

    Sandipani Hostel

    The primary residence for freshmen, focusing on discipline and integration into campus life.

    BLOCK 2

    Bhartrihari Hostel

    A spacious block featuring double-seater rooms and immediate access to the playground.

    BLOCK 3

    Kalidas Hostel

    Specifically designated for senior engineering students and PG scholars.

    BLOCK 4

    Vikramaditya Hostel

    Equipped with a modern reading room and internal recreational facilities.

    BLOCK 5

    Varahamihira Hostel

    A quiet residential wing dedicated to research scholars and PhD candidates.

    Girls' Hostels (Separate Complex)

    MAIN BLOCK

    Vidyottama Hostel

    A highly secure zone with 24/7 CCTV surveillance and an in-house medical room.

    ANNEX

    Avantika Girls Hostel

    Features green courtyards for study and leisure, specifically for undergraduate students.

    PG WING

    Gargi Hostel

    Premium block offering single-occupancy rooms for post-graduate and research students.

    Campus Dining Facilities

    Central Boys Mess

    Large capacity dining hall serving nutritious, pure vegetarian meals for all male residents.

    Vidyottama Girls Mess

    Exclusive dining hall for female students with a focus on hygiene and balanced diets.

    Cooperative Mess

    Student-managed facility where residents collaborate on the menu and quality control.

    Quick Bite Cafeteria

    A daytime facility providing snacks and beverages for non-resident students and visitors.

    Hostel Amenities

    • 24/7 Purified Water (RO)
    • Power Backup & Solar Geysers
    • High-Speed Wi-Fi Connectivity
    • Daily Cleaning & Security Guards

    Admissions Notice

    Hostel seats are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis according to university merit lists. Applicants must submit a separate hostel application form during the counseling process.
